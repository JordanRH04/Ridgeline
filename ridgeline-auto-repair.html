If you proceed with the repair at Ridge Line, we waive the diagnostic fee — it gets credited toward your total. You only pay the $49 if you decide not to move forward with us, which is your right. No pressure either way.
Oil changes take 30–45 minutes. Brake pad replacements run 1–2 hours depending on the vehicle. Most single-axle brake jobs are done same day if you drop off in the morning. We'll always give you a time estimate before we start.
